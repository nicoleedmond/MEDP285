Great Food
nav
Pizza. Classic and customizable. Nothing beats a good slice of bread and cheese.
Sushi. My favorite is a salmon avacado roll and crispy rice. Fresh, light, and full of flavor.
Tacos. Street-style or fully loaded, tacos are always a good idea.
Pasta. Comfort food at its finest. Spaghetti, pappardelle, capellini, fettuccine...I'll have them all.
Pancakes. Fluffy breakfast essential. Best with butter, syrup, and maybe some fresh fruit.
Mac and Cheese. Warm, cheesy, and nostalgic. Nothing compares.
Ramen. Flavor-packed broth, noodles, and toppings, what's not to love.
Quinoa Bowl. High in protein and gluten-free. Love to pair this with chicken or steak and top it with veggies and a light dressing.
Grilled Salmon. Rich in omega-3's, great for brain and heart health.
Greek Yogurt. Packed with probiotics and antioxidants. A drizzle of honey and some chia seeds spice this up.
Empanadas. My favorite is filled with meat and potatoes.
Chocolate Chip Cookies. Can't forget dessert. Homemade is the best.
