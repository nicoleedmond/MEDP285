Fall’s Embrace

The trees are in their autumn
beauty, The woodland paths are dry, Under the
October twilight the water
Mirrors a still sky.
-William Butler Yeats

Winter’s Whisper

When the geese are flying south And the sky
is grey, my dears, Close your eyes and
lift your nose; Listen with your careful ears.
Feel the winter coming on; Hear it in the
crackling trees; Note the crisping,
quivering wind
Sharply snapping at their leaves.
-Barbara Vance
