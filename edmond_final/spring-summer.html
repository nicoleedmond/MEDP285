Spring Awakening

How can I love this spring when it's
pulling me through my life faster than
any time before it? When five separate dooms
promised this decade and here I am,
just trying to watch a bumblebee cling to its
first purple flower. I cannot save this world.
But look how it's trying, once again, to save me.
-James A. Pearson

Summer Radiance

It feels cruel. Something in me
isn't ready to let go of summer so easily.
To destroy what I've carefully
cultivated all these months.
Those pale flowers might still have time to fruit.
-Karina Borowicz
